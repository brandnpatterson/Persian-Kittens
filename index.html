About Us

We are a small hobby CFA registered cattery specializing in Himalayans Cats (Persians with Points) located near Houston, Texas. We raise sweet, healthy, fully socialized, litter box trained fur~babies. My breeding cats are PKD/DNA FELV & FIV NEG. Our cattery and cats are also free of fleas, fungus and internal parasites.

We show in both CFA and TICA. We breed for type, head as well as body specifics to ensure consistency with CFA and TICA standards. We only produce a couple litters each year and will sell our cats as PETS ONLY~ We give NO BREEDING RIGHTS and we DO NOT CO~OWN or and DO NOT OFFER stud services.
